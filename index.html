About the league

A local league built for speed, strategy, and community.

Ultimate Capture the Flag brings organized Capture the Flag to Cypress, Texas with a high-energy league structure families can trust. Players learn teamwork, communication, leadership, athleticism, and strategy while competing in a safe, well-run environment.

The experience is designed to be exciting for teens and young adults, approachable for new players, and dependable for parents who want clear expectations and strong supervision.

Strategy in motion

Players read the field, coordinate routes, defend zones, and make split-second decisions.

Community-minded

Families, teammates, volunteers, and referees help create a positive league culture.

Rules & safety

Competitive energy, clear expectations.

Capture the Flag is fast and strategic. Ultimate Capture the Flag keeps the game organized with rules, referees, field boundaries, and sportsmanship standards.

  • Respect all players and teams
  • Respect referees and field staff
  • Stay within field boundaries
  • No dangerous contact
  • Proper athletic attire required
  • Hydration encouraged
  • Parent permission required for minors
  • Good sportsmanship expected
